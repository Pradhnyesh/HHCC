ğŸ“– Our Story

Founded in 2009 by a group of passionate community members, Helping Hands Community Care began as a small daycare center with a big dream. Our founders, Sarah Martinez and Dr. James Wilson, recognized the growing need for comprehensive, family-centered care services in our community.

What started as a 20-child daycare has grown into a full-service community care center serving over 500 families. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core mission: treating every person in our care like family and providing services that make a real difference in people's lives.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as the premier care provider in our community, with numerous awards for excellence in care, innovation in programming, and outstanding community service.
